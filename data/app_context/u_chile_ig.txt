START JOB DESCRIPTION

The Center for Applied Economics (http://www.cea-uchile.cl/) of the Department of Industrial Engineering at the University of Chile is currently inviting applications for academic positions at both the assistant and associate professor levels. We invite applicants in all fields of Economics. Applicants should have a PhD (or be close to completion) in Economics or related fields and be highly committed to academic research. Knowledge of Spanish is not required but we expect successful applicants to be able to teach in Spanish within two years following the appointment.

Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis.

END JOB DESCRIPTION

START INSTITUTIONAL DESCRIPTION

The University of Chile, founded in 1842, is Chile's oldest higher education institution. Key characteristics:

• Mission: Generate, develop, integrate and communicate knowledge across all areas of knowledge and culture
• Public university with international quality standards
• Comprehensive coverage of all knowledge areas through teaching, research, creation and outreach
• Top-ranked institution in the Chilean university system
• 16 faculties and 3 interdisciplinary institutes across five campuses
• 47,307 students (2024)


END INSTITUTIONAL DESCRIPTION
