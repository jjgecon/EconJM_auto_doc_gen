START ECON DEPT

- The Department of Applied Economics (DEA) at UAB is committed to excellence in research and teaching across all major fields of applied economics, including Public Economics, Labour Economics, International Economics, Environmental and Resource Economics, Urban and Regional Economics, Transport Economics, Rural Development, Applied Macroeconomics, and Applied Microeconomics.
- The DEA fosters a collaborative research environment with around 20 full-time members, a vibrant community of graduate and postgraduate students, and frequent international visitors.
- The department actively participates in international research networks and projects, with strong links to European research initiatives and funding.
- DEA faculty are engaged in teaching at both undergraduate and graduate levels, running a recognized PhD program and several Master’s programs, including the International Master’s Programme (IMP).
- The department values internationalization, regularly hiring from the global job market and encouraging faculty mobility.
- Potential co-authors and research groups include Cervini-Plá, M (gender economics) and Rosella Nicolini (language and culture), offering strong opportunities for collaboration in these areas.
