START ECON DEPT
- The IPP is a partnership between the Paris School of Economics (PSE) and GENES/CREST, bringing together researchers from PSE, CREST, and other institutions.
- Research topics span taxation, social and environmental policy, labor markets, health, education, pensions, housing, democracy, institutions, and competition policy.
- Researchers benefit from a collaborative environment with opportunities for co-authorship across PSE, CREST, and affiliated teams.
- PSE hosts 140 researchers and 200 PhD students, offering advanced teaching and fostering interdisciplinary exchange.
- GENES/CREST unites faculty from ENSAE Paris, ENSAI, Ecole Polytechnique Economics Department, and CNRS, supporting joint research and teaching initiatives.
