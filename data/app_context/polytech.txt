START INSTITUTIONAL DESCRIPTION

- Institut Polytechnique de Paris (IP Paris) aims to deliver world-class education and cutting-edge research in science and technology.
- The institution values multidisciplinary collaboration, drawing on the strengths of six prestigious engineering schools.
- IP Paris is committed to innovation, knowledge transfer, and cultivating inventiveness through its dynamic ecosystem and incubators.
- It emphasizes excellence, selectivity, and adaptability in its educational programs to prepare graduates for a competitive global job market.
- The institute fosters diversity, internationalization, and the development of high-potential doctoral students through ambitious PhD programs.
- IP Paris addresses major economic and social challenges via interdisciplinary research centers and partnerships with industry.


END INSTITUTIONAL DESCRIPTION

START ECON DEPT

- The Economics Department (CREST) emphasizes quantitative methods, mathematical modeling, and the integration of empirical analysis to address economic and social issues.
- Main research areas include economics, finance, statistics, actuarial sciences, and sociology.
- CREST is an interdisciplinary unit with members from CNRS, GENES, École Polytechnique, and Télécom Paris, fostering collaboration across institutions.
- Research and teaching groups focus on digital economics, cultural consumption, gender studies, and natural language processing.
- Potential co-authors include:
    - Samuel Coavoux (digital uses and cultural consumption)
    - Marion Gousse (gender studies)
    - Etienne Ollion (natural language processing)

END ECON DEPT
