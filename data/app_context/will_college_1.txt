START ECON DEPT

- The Williams Economics Department values both teaching and research excellence, with a diverse and innovative curriculum.
- Faculty are active scholars, fostering a collaborative environment for research and teaching.
- The department is a leader among liberal arts colleges, with strong interdisciplinary ties (e.g., Political Economy program, Center for Development Economics).
