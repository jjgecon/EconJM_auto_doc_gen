- Research strengths include labour economics, macroeconomics, political economy, and public economics.
- The department is active in teaching at all levels, with around 1000 students annually.
- Uppsala Center for Labor Studies (UCLS) focuses on the Swedish labour market, globalisation, immigration, and technological change.
