START JOB DESCRIPTION
- Seeking candidates in Macroeconomics, Microeconomic Theory, Applied Microeconomic Theory, International Trade, and Development Economics.
- Appointments at tenure-track Assistant/Associate Professor or tenured Associate Professor level.
- Candidates must have (or soon complete) a PhD in Economics; tenure-track applicants should have earned their PhD no earlier than 2021.
- Evidence of excellence in research at the highest international level is required (innovative job market paper, publications, strong references, etc.).
- Demonstrated excellence in teaching, supported by a teaching dossier and evaluations.
- Ability to complement and enhance departmental research strengths; motivation for applying should address fit with department profile.
- For tenured positions, proficiency in Norwegian (B2 level within two years) is required.
- Some positions linked to externally funded projects (WaCoMacro, EXPLOIT, NORCIE); candidates should indicate relevant contributions.
- Commitment to diversity and gender balance; open to candidates with varied backgrounds and perspectives.

END JOB DESCRIPTION
