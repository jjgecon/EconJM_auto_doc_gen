START ECON DEPT

- The Center for Economic Studies (CEE) at El Colegio de México is committed to excellence in research and teaching in economics, with a strong tradition in graduate education (Master’s and PhD) and a recently established undergraduate program.
- Faculty members are internationally trained, most are part of Mexico’s National System of Researchers (SNI), and the department regularly hosts visiting scholars from Mexico and abroad.
- Research topics include macroeconomics, population studies, urbanization, and a broad range of economics fields, with frequent publication of international articles, books, and working papers.
- The CEE fosters collaboration through seminars, conferences, and workshops, including major international events (e.g., LACEA LAMES, Society for Social Choice and Welfare).
- Potential co-authors and research/teaching groups include Pablo Soto-Mota, whose work focuses on ethics and institutions.
