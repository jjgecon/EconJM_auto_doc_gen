START ECON DEPT

- ThEMA is a joint research centre in economics and management science, affiliated with the University of Cergy-Pontoise and CNRS, and includes faculty from ESSEC Business School.
- The centre values collaboration, interdisciplinary research, and engagement with economic policy issues.
- Research topics include theoretical and applied microeconomics (game theory, decision theory, social choice, general equilibrium, development, family, inequality, transport, taxation, education, competition policy), macroeconomics, labour and international trade, finance, and marketing.
- ThEMA is part of Labex MME-DII, supporting advanced research in mathematical and economic modeling of dynamics, uncertainty, and interactions.
- The department comprises around 60 researchers, 40+ PhD students, and 5 administrative staff.
- Potential co-authors and research groups: e.g., Azar Salim (gender in business), and other faculty specializing in the above fields.
