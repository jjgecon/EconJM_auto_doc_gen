Institute of Economics and Econometrics

- The Institute of Economics and Econometrics focuses on frontier research and teaching in economics and econometrics.
- Key research areas include labor economics, development and environmental economics, international economics and trade, urban economics, and advanced econometric methods.
- Faculty are internationally recognized and publish in top general and field journals.
- The Institute collaborates with major international organizations in Geneva and globally, offering opportunities for joint research and internships.
- Weekly seminars and conferences foster an active research environment.
- Researchers advise governments and organizations on topics such as inequality, poverty, unemployment, human development, and globalization.
- Potential co-authors include Jérémy LUCCHETTI (conflict research) and Salvatore DI FALCO (social and religion punishments).
