START ECON DEPT

- The Department of Economics is part of the AACSB-accredited College of Business and Economics at UW-Whitewater.
- Research and teaching emphasize applied analysis and empirical methods, with strengths in Development Economics, Microeconomics of Banking, Environmental Economics, and Machine Learning Methods.
- The department supports three degree programs (BBA, BA/BS, BSE) and has 16 full-time faculty and staff, providing opportunities for collaboration and co-authorship.
- The College of Business and Economics is the largest AACSB-accredited business school in Wisconsin, enrolling ~3,500 undergraduates and ~1,000 graduate students across 11 majors and several master's/doctoral programs.
- Faculty and students benefit from a collaborative environment with diverse research and teaching groups, especially in applied microeconomics and data-driven fields.
